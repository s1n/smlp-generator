You can become a new member of NASH or renew your NASH membership here, either online or by printing the NASH Membership Application. Select the desired membership or donation, then complete the information at the bottom of the page.

To print the application, download the NASH Membership Application by clicking here, then print, fill-out and send the form to NASH, along with the appropriate membership fee.

To join online, complete the information below. Find the membership level that you want and follow the simple instructions.

Read about member benefits by clicking here.

There are several membership levels:

Registered Membership is open to all members certified by the Council for Homeopathic Certification. Registration entitles members to use the prestigious designation RSHom(NA), access to the legal defense fund, listings in the online and published directories, full voting rights, free promotional materials, The American Homeopath journal and more.
Registered Membership, $250 annually
To apply for NEW Registered Membership, download the application below and complete according to the instructions. Then mail the application, application fee ($40) together with any applicable documentation to NASH. Online Registered Membership is not available to new members at this time.
