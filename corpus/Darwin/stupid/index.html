The stupidity displayed by the participants
in the following tales stops short of the ultimate
sacrifice, but we salute their spirit and innovation.

The new millennium begins inauspiciously with I Shot the Sheriff, Wild Shot, and more men killed by guns, sheep, electricity, skiing, and thieving cavern crystal stalactites. Can life get more exciting? Yes! Janitors try to exterminate a gopher, men jump trash chutes and sleep in garbage bins, and chimneys make more than one appearance. Mr. Happy meets Mr. Vacuum, and dozens more! A man staples his <ahem> to a cross, criminals are imbeciles, and duct tape, blondes, and lawn chairs all play their improbable parts.
