Search Tips

Look at the Site Map.

Use 'or' and 'not' to modify searches.
A search for "gun" retrieves 16 links, while "gun not police" gives 10. A search for "australia" retrieves 7 links, while "australia or germany" gives 12.

Truncate words using *
"drain*" retrieves 4 links, while "drainpipe" gets none.

Unusual Characters
The search retains capitalization for all characters other than [a-z A-Z] so the word "Çelik" is not retrieved by "çelik", "Celik", or "celik". You can use words containing any entity from ! (#033) to ÿ (#255).
